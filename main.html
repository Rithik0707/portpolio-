To avoid these problems this system designed with the help of Raspberry Pi. The digital image processing plays important role in the sign capturing and detection system. In this project lightweight techniques were mainly used due to limitation of real time-based applications and raspberry Pi capabilities. Web Camera is used to capture the road signs with image enhancement techniques the embedded systems small computing platform studies the characteristics of speed and road signs.
